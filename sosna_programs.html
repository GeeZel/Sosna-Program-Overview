1. Sunzi - 21-Day Rehabilitation & Guiding Program

A comprehensive 21-day healing journey designed to restore balance and well-being. Structured in three levels:

  • Level 1: For those in a challenging or unhealthy state, just beginning their healing journey
  • Level 2: For individuals looking to readjust and realign their health
  • Level 3: For those with a healthy lifestyle who need support in creating and maintaining new habits

Includes:

  • One-on-One Therapy: Tailored to your individual needs, addressing specific areas of focus
  • Group Sessions: Heal in community, where shared experiences and collective wisdom support personal growth
  • Family Sessions: Engaging with loved ones to create a healing environment that supports lasting change

2. Tatsu - Immersive Mind-Body Connection Program

Helps individuals strengthen the connection between their mind, body, and spirit through a variety of physical and emotional techniques. This program focuses on personal transformation by promoting mindfulness, emotional balance, and body awareness through tailored practices.

Program Structure:

  • Mind-Body Integration: Techniques to integrate emotional balance and body healing
  • Practical Mindfulness: Practices that incorporate deep mindfulness into daily routines
  • Body Awareness: Physical techniques like gentle movement and breathwork

Includes:

  • One-on-One Therapy: Personal guidance to align your mind and body in harmony
  • Group Sessions: Engage with others to practice mindfulness and body awareness together
  • Family Sessions: Encourage mindfulness in the family dynamic, promoting collective healing

3. Nagomi - Holistic Stress Reduction & Relaxation Program

Designed to help individuals manage stress, cultivate relaxation, and restore inner peace. Through a variety of holistic techniques, Nagomi supports emotional, mental, and physical well-being, promoting deep relaxation and resilience to stress in everyday life.

Program Structure:

  • Stress Management Techniques: Learn to identify and release stress through deep relaxation methods
  • Mindfulness & Meditation: Practices that help quiet the mind and bring you into the present moment
  • Body Relaxation: Gentle movement, breathwork, and other methods to relieve tension and promote calmness

Includes:

  • One-on-One Therapy: Personalized relaxation techniques that work best for your needs
  • Group Sessions: Experience relaxation practices together, creating a peaceful and supportive environment
  • Family Sessions: Helping families integrate relaxation and stress reduction into their daily lives for collective healing

4. Gezi - Personalized Life Journey & Habit-Tailoring Program

A deeply personalized healing journey focused on habit formation and alignment with your life's goals. This program provides individualized support and coaching to transform your habits, helping you cultivate a life that supports long-term health and happiness.

Program Structure:

  • Personalized Life Assessment: Understand your life goals, challenges, and current habits
  • Habit-Tailoring: Practical tools and strategies for building sustainable habits
  • Ongoing Support: Continuous personalized coaching to ensure you maintain progress

Includes:

  • One-on-One Therapy: Tailored support to guide you through your personal transformation
  • Group Sessions: Shared experiences for mutual support and learning
  • Family Sessions: Helping families build healthy habits and navigate life changes together
